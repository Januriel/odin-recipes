Why “Food of Teyvat”?

For those who don’t know, Teyvat is the name of the world in the game Genshin Impact. As someone who plays Genshin Impact semi-religiously (and have gotten quite obsessed with collecting recipes and dishes in-game), I decided that I must flaunt my love for genshin food here by featuring them on site.


Fun Facts:

  • All recipes featured on “Food of Teyvat” are picked using the Genshin Impact Wiki as reference and a number generator.
  • Coincidentally, the 4 recipes are respectively from Mondstadt, Inazuma, Liyue and Sumeru, the four playable nations of Genshin Impact as of March 2023.
