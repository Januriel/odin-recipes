Butter Crab

Description

A grilled crab dish. Appropriate heat control has allowed the crab meat to stay moist and fatty. The butter has seeped into the crab meat, producing an aroma that has people salivating even from afar.


Ingredients (4 servings):

  • 2 lbs. mud crab, dungeness crab or stone crab
  • 1 1/2 cup evaporated milk
  • 1 stick butter
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons water
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  • 2 stalks curry leaves
  • 5 bird's eye chilies, lightly pounded

Steps:

  1. Clean the crab and cut into pieces. Use paper towels to pat dry the crab.
  2. Mix the cornstarch with water and set aside. Heat up a wok to medium heat. Melt the butter in the wok before adding the curry leaves and bird's eyes chilies. As soon as you smell the aroma from the curry leaves and bird's eye chilies, add in the crab and stir continuously until the crab starts to turn colour.
  3. Add the evaporated milk and cover the wok and turn the heat to low. Simmer for about 5 minutes, and then add in cornstarch to thicken the creamy butter sauce. Dish out and serve hot and the fried mantou.
