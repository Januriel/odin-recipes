Crab Roe Tofu

Description

A dish with a tender mouthfeel. Crab roe is stir-fried till the oil within oozes out before being added to boiled tofu and accented with broth. Simple, yes, but gloriously fresh.


Ingredients (1 serving):

  • 1 box soft tofu (abt 700g), cubed
  • 300g homemade crab roe powder
  • 10g chives, chopped finely
  • 5g salt
  • 5g cornstarch
  • 30g lard

Steps:

  1. Mix cornstarch and water to form slurry. Melt lard in the heated pan before adding the crab roe powder. Mix until fragrant. Add tofu, salt and about 200 ml water to the pan.
  2. Cook on medium heat for 6 - 8 minutes to let tofu absorb the flavour of crab roe.
  3. Add slurry to the mixture. When the liquids have thickened, garnish with chives and serve while hot.



"My Way"

Description

Yanfei's specialty is Crab Roe Tofu done to an incredible degree of regularity. Its dimensions and flavor are all faithfully rendered according to the "Crab Roe Tofu Laws" in the recipe, with not a single letter omitted.


Ingredients (1 serving):

  • 1 box soft tofu (abt 700g), cubed
  • 300g homemade crab roe powder
  • 10g chives, chopped finely
  • 5g salt
  • 30g lard

Steps:

  1. Melt lard in a heated pan and add 200g of the crab roe powder. Mix until fragrant before being removed from heat onto a dish.
  2. Place tofu on the crab roe mixture. Sprinkle some salt and lightly brush with crab roe oil. Steam for 10 minutes.
  3. Heat the remaining 100g crab roe on pan to release the aroma.
  4. Arrange the crab roe in a circle on the steamed tofu. Garnish with chives.
