Chicken Mushroom Skewers

Description

A skewer of mushrooms and poultry. Fresh poultry is complimented by fragrant mushrooms. Don't be picky, chow down!


Ingredients (3 servings):

  • 2 chicken thighs, deboned
  • 3 fresh shiitake mushrooms
  • Oil (abt 5g)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)

Steps:

  1. Cut the deboned chicken thighs into 3 pieces each. Remove stems from shiitake mushrooms.
  2. Marinate the meat in oil, salt and pepper.
  3. Preheat the grill.
  4. Assemble the ingredients with 2 pieces of meat with 1 mushroom on each skewer. Roll up the chicken thighs when skewering.
  5. Cook skewers on the grill until the meat is thoroughly cooked.



Fruity Skewers

Description

Kaeya's specialty. A chewy skewer with a rich sweet aftertaste. Wait...Is this really the smell of fruit?


Ingredients (6 servings):

  • 4 chicken thighs, deboned
  • 6 fresh shiitake mushrooms
  • ½ zucchini, diced
  • ½ onion, diced
  • 30ml white sparkling wine
  • Oil (abt 8g)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)

Steps:

  1. Cut the deboned chicken thighs into 3 pieces each. Remove stems from shiitake mushrooms.
  2. Marinate the meat in oil, white sparkling wine, salt and pepper.
  3. Preheat the grill.
  4. Assemble the ingredients with 2 pieces of meat with 1 mushroom, zucchini and onion slice on each skewer. Roll up the chicken thighs when skewering.
  5. Cook skewers on the grill until the meat is thoroughly cooked.
